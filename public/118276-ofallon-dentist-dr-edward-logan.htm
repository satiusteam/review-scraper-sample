Meet the Doctor

Using the latest dental technologies and cosmetic dentistry procedures, Dr. Logan can help you create the smile that you deserve. Dr. Logan offers gentle, quality care for the entire family that is affordable and most importantly pain free. The friendly staff is always happy to answer any of your questions regarding all aspects of your dental needs. Dr. Logan is experienced in cosmetic dentistry and general dentistry. His office enjoys treating patients of all ages. Most insurances are accepted and financing is available.
